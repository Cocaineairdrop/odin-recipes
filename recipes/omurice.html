Omurice


Description

Omurice is a Japanese dish that merges an omelet with fried rice. It’s also known as omuraisu, a blend of “omelet” and “rice” in Japanese, or omumeshi, which directly translates to “rice.”

Ingredients

  • 2 cups prepared white rice – minute rice works fine
  • ½ cup diced or shredded chicken
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil – divided
  • ¼ white or yellow onion – diced
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • ½ cup frozen vegetable mix peas/carrots/corn – defrosted
  • 4 large eggs
  • salt and pepper – to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
